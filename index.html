African Elephant

African Elephant is the largest land animal on Earth. It is known for its intelligence, strong social bonds, and large ears that help regulate body temperature. These animals are found in various parts of Africa, including savannas and forests. I like elephants because they are gentle, powerful, and highly intelligent creatures. They play an important role in maintaining the ecosystem by spreading seeds and creating pathways in defense forests

Interesting Facts

  • Elephants are the largest land animals
  • They have excellent memory
  • They use their trunks to communicate and grab objects
